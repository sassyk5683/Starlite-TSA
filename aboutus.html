About Us

Starlite is founded by two women in STEM and is a very employee-driven company. Our mission is to discover the unknown of space while making sure that every astronaut, staff, and crew member is taken care of and feels safe. As space can be very intimidating, Starlite is here to guide you through your process by informing, advising, and giving love and care every step of the way. If you have any questions, concerns, or want to join us please contact us!

Future Plans of Company

Starlite plans to expand in the near future. The company is working on creating new space vehicles for astronauts to explore with advance technologies. Also researching and exploring more expeditions options for astronauts so they can unlock more of the unknown world. Starlite also wants to be more safer by coming up with more secure ways to launch and recover astronauts and cargo from space. There are also future planning on advertising the company more by creating an informational learning center for the public. The learning center would come with fun interactives, inform other people about Starlite, merchandise centers, and most importantly be family friendly. There will also be more advertising to the locals as the company is planning on creating billboards as well as fun flyers to be passed around.
