Company Starships

Accolades

Our newest ship with the most advanced technology. Not only is the ship designed to bring out the best in our astronauts but it's also designed for comfort and luxury. The Accolades has many research stations on board as well as one-of-a-kind boarding rooms and mess halls.

Sparta X

Sparta X was the second ship in our fleet. This ship is perfect for research and discovery. It has the biggest research station out of our other ships in the fleet and good cargo space for foundings.

Kayis

Kayis is the oldest ship in our fleet however, it still has the quality of all our other ships. The Kayis was Starlight's first and only ship when we started. The Kayis has recently been renovated with newer technology yet it still has a classic luxury style.
