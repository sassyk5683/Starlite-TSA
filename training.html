Training We Provide

Classes

At Starlite, classes and exams are provided to ensure you are qualified to become an astronaut. 90% of attendence is required while also all classes and exams must be completed with passing grade, 75 or higher. This is a requirement that has to be completed, unless you already have experience in the field.

Physical Training

To prepare your body and mind for your journey in space, Starlite has simulations and equipment that try to give the closest experience of space. Paperwork of consent is required to go through. You may NOT interact with any simulation until given paperwork is turned in.


Land and Sea Survival Simulation

A simulation that tests your ability to survive on land and sea.


Scuba Diving

Trip out to the sea to learn what it feels like with little oxygen.


Space Suits

Get accustom to how our space suits feel and practice doing tasks.


Weightlessness Simulation

A simulation where you can experience the motions of zero gravity.


Flight training with Flight Simulations

Learn how to drive flying vechicles like planes than start with Starlite vechiles.
